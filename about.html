About Me

Hello! My name is Roshun Sunder. I'm currently student at Columbia University studying Computer Science and Economics. I've explored an array of diverse interests – from FinTech, to app development, to data science – and am always looking for a way to channel my curiosity.

Both in and out of the classroom, I've found a love for learning, whether it is new skills or exploring technologies that I'm familiar with more deeply. My past experiences as a Fullstack Software Engineering intern and project creator are a reflection of my passion for building things; I frequently find myself asking "how can I make this better?" or "what can we create to help optimize this?" (oftentimes ending up with more ideas than I can keep track of).

A natural byproduct of my passion and eagerness to learn is a constant search for interesting projects to work on or products to build. I'm fascinated by the way that technology intersects with other fields, and am always on a quest to bring creativity into my work. If you have something in mind, you can reach me at roshun.sunder@gmail.com!

Or, you know, we can just chat too. I'm always in the mood to meet new people :)
